About Me

I am Trisha Sarkar, hailing from Chandraganj, Lakshmipur. Currently, I am a dedicated student at Jahangirnagar University, where I am pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Engineering. I am in my 4th semester and am deeply passionate about my field of study. My aspiration is to become a professor at a renowned university, where I can contribute to the academic community and inspire the next generation of computer scientists. I consistently strive to put in my best effort to achieve this dream. Whether it's through excelling in my coursework, engaging in research projects, or participating in extracurricular activities, I am committed to my goal of becoming an educator. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am keen on staying updated with the latest advancements in technology and education. I believe in continuous learning and self-improvement, and I actively seek out opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills. I am also involved in various student organizations and enjoy collaborating with my peers on innovative projects. Through hard work, perseverance, and a passion for teaching, I am determined to reach my dream of becoming a professor and making a meaningful impact in the field of computer science education.
